Andreaa's Snapchat

Custom AR Filters & Creative Lens Experiences

"Bringing joy and creativity to Snapchat! Custom AR filters crafted with artistic vision and augmented reality technology."

Andreaa's Snapchat is a collection of custom AR filters and creative lens experiences designed to bring joy, fun, and creativity to Snapchat users. Each filter is crafted with attention to detail, blending artistic vision with augmented reality technology. This Snapchat Heart Filter was created during my Rookie to Bashers selection—it adds floating hearts over your face, giving a fun and lively touch to selfies. The filter can be downloaded and saved to local storage for later use.
